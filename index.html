How to find out my Ticket
  1. Play game
  2. Open console (Developer tools/ Ctrl+Shift+I)
  3. Target gameiframe
  4. Type PlayFab._internalSettings.sessionTicket
  5. Press enter. The red text is your ticket (quotes exclude). It expired in 24 hours.
